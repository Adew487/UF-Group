About Us

UF Group Of Companies

UF Group, established in the early ’90s, is a privately-owned Group of companies progressing in the service sector of Pakistan. Transport and Logistics is the core business of the group and is also renowned in the textile export sector.

At UF Group we are ready to put ourselves to the test and demonstrate how our passion for solutions can contribute to the success of your business. We adopt our customer worries and are flexible enough to meet customer needs and provide tailor-made solutions to minimize logistics cost and lead time. A number of our satisfied customers including Multinational and leading industrial groups are working with us for more than a decade. In their eyes, we have become an integral business partner. We are extremely proud of the progress and growth that the organization has achieved during the past years.
